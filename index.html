Feed a Star-Nosed Mole

A cute browser game powered on pure JavaScript

Based off the hit classic Whack-a-Mole! Play as Tweety the Bird, and feed cute, starving, and ever impatient (!) star-nosed moles. Fill up Willy the Worm Meter, as you fill up these cute little moles by feeding them worm treats. There's plenty of worms to go around so keep everyone happy!

Dship Psalms Tracker

Tracking 150 days for 150 Psalms

An efficient Java program that uses bible-api.com and JSON objects to track a daily bible reading plan through the book of Psalms. Automatically updates and fetches new psalm each day in KJV.

Look at Me Mom, I Can Cook (LAMMIC)

Meal Prep, Recipe Book, and Health Tracking All in One

An Android application a couple friends and I built for SD Hacks 2019. Perfect for college students to budget and prep meals, learn to cook, as well track dietary habits. Focus on eating healthy, living nutritiously, and most importantly, up your cooking game with a variety of simple to gourmet recipes.
